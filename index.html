SKATEBOARDING

Skateboarding has effected my life in so many ways, but mainly changing the way I go about problems. Instead of being ashamed I failed, I get back up and strive to do better. Skating has changed my mentality to be much better, failing for me isn't a bad thing, its a step to eventually succeed. I want to talk about skating and share my experiences with it.

Culture

Street Skating became a icon of non-comformity, doing what the people want. With its heavy roots in punk at the start. Skating was rebelious.

Tricks

Skate tricks are a integral part of skating, from freestyle to street there is a very large variety of tricks Skaters chose from. Although specifc tricks may be frowned upon it doesn't stop people from doing what they want.

Fashion

Skate fashion can vary heavily, but it mainly takes inspiration from some of the first Street-Skaters who wore baggy clothing. Baggy clothing also helps prevent scrapes/scratches and provides comfort when stretching and doing specific tricks.

TRICKS

There are a WIDE variety of Skateboarding tricks, as not only can you do a trick one way, but you can do Nollie, Switch, and Fakie variations of every trick. aswell as doing multiple in succession (Ex: Double Flip, Double Varial, 900's, etc). Some tricks that are commonly known are: Kickflip, Ollie, and the famous Tony Hawk 900.
